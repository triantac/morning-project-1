Contact Info For Health Providers

For more information, consult the Greek National Public Health Organisation (NPHO) via telephone (dial 1135, or 210 521 2054, from within Greece). The NPHO can provide contact details of a doctor; their telephone line operates 24 hours a day.

If you think you have symptoms, including a fever or respiratory difficulties such as shortness of breath or a cough, you should avoid visiting local health facilities, but contact a doctor remotely to see whether a test is recommended.


In case of emergency while staying in the EU, call 112.
If you need an ambulance in Greece, call 166.
