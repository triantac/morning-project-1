We are back to living in Greece safely and responsibly! Welcome to “Safe Living & Travelling GR”. Our purpose is to inform you on all recent guidelines regarding the COVID-19 pandemic. In this website, you will find a variety of resources for multiple groups of people, including high-risk citizens, employers, and travellers. The information and statistics provided have been validated by health institutions and are updated regularly. Considering the importance of mental health and the impact of the pandemic on the mental state of the entire world, we are also providing information for mental support, as well as resources for children, including fairytales, videos, and posters. Contact details and general information is also available and we are open to suggestions, corrections, as well as to your questions or concerns. Stay calm, safe, healthy, and well-informed!
